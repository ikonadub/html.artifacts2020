The Best Movies According to Isabella

My Top 3 Movies.

Night at The Museaum

This is my favourite funny movie. It reminds me of when I was a kid and dreamed of being an archeologist, I also have a great appreciation of historical things.

Dragon Ball Z

Best anime. I enjoy the storyline in the initial persuit to defend the earth. The adventure, is a big journey to take on nearly is impossible. The confidence, leadership, and bravery in this story reminds me of myself.

Pretty Woman

Super beneficial movie created before its time. To see how far integrity, lust and, trust, will go is good to have a realistic view that these things happen in real life, but at the cost of everything .
